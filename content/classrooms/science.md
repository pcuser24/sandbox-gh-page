Welcome to our core laboratory experience. In this class, students don't just read about equations; they prove them. 

### What We Cover Each Week
1. **Kinematics:** Launching model projectiles to calculate acceleration.
2. **Exothermic Reactions:** Studying energy states through safe, highly-visual chemical reactions.
3. **Circuit Work:** Building basic parallel circuits to power custom hardware modules.

### Requirements
All safety gear (goggles, coats) is provided in the classroom laboratory. No prior advanced math skills required.
